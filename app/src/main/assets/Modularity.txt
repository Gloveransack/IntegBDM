			<p>The Integ design philosophy is based around simplicity and&nbsp;modularity.</p>
<p>Simplicity translates into reliability of the product.&nbsp; Once set
 up, the product will maintain its position until it is moved.</p>
<p>Modularity refers to the <strong>'building-blocks'</strong> style that allows many configurations using a standard set of components.</p>
<p>This also allows future upgrades to be added on, for just the cost of the additional parts.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #00ccff;">Apollo System</span></h3>
<p><strong>1st Building Block: the base</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Desk Mount - edge clamp, or clamp through hole</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Wall Mount - screwed directly onto wall or attached to partition screen</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Floor Mount - free standing or floor to ceiling post.</p>

			<p><strong>2nd Building Block: the post</strong></p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">All Apollo posts share the proprietary 
slot,&nbsp;from single-slot through to 6-slot configurations.&nbsp; This
 commonality allows the same connector to fit any Apollo post.&nbsp; 
Multiple slots allow multiple attachments to slide past each other, or 
be fixed at the same height,&nbsp;on the post.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">The Post can be any length to suit your requirement.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">The Post can include sockets for power/usb/phone/internet.</p>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">Integ Posts:</p>
<ul>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo1 - Single slot, wall mount only</li>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo2 wall - Double slot, wall mount only</li>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo2 pole - Double slot, desk mount only</li>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo4 - 4 slots, desk mount only</li>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo4 Power+ - 4 slots, desk mount and wall mount, allows power and data fittings as well as internal cable management</li>
<li style="padding-left: 30px;">Apollo6 - 6 slots, desk mount, wall 
mount and freestanding mount, allows power and data fittings as well as 
internal cable management</li>
</ul>
