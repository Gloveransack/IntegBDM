Apollo4 Monitor Arms

Our classic model

 

When we first set out to design a monitor arm, we wanted to address the on-going issues of form & function. We also wanted to design a product that would stand the test of time.  The simplicity in design of our Apollo4 range and its ability to expand as your needs change continues to make this range as popular as ever.

Four (4) slots, lots of possibilities

The key component to the Apollo4 is a pole with four (4) unique grooves (the Apollo slot) which connects the arm(s) and accessories to the mounting mechanism.  Four (4) slots means up to four monitor or accessory arms can be added. The possibilities are almost endless with laptop trays, phone trays and multiple monitor arms able to be added at any point during the life of an installation.

This slot ensures the monitor arms needs only one tool to install (a factory supplied Allen key) or adjust and it contains no mechanical parts that can break down.

More usable space

Free up valuable desk space with the Apollo4 Monitor Arm. Using a monitor arm can help to provide up to 25% more desk space with a footprint that is a fraction of older CRT screens, plastic stands or docking stations.

With a truly flexible arm the location of the monitor depends on the nature of your work.

 

Ergonomic Benefits

The Apollo4 monitor arm allows your monitor to be placed at any height with the re-assurance that the arm will not drupe or weep over time ensuring you maintain a comfortable work environment.

If you need to adjust the monitor on a regular basis then our shuttle can easily be added to the product providing even greater flexibility.

  

Apollo4 with Apollo limbs and shuttle

Increase Productivity by 44%

Research indicates that the use of dual screens can increase productivity by 44% (sometimes even more) making two monitors better than one.

Many new desktop computers support dual monitors without requiring any additional hardware. Why stop at two screens. With our Apollo4, you can mount up to four screens all with our factory supplied Allen key (Hex key).
