Apollo4 Power+ (Internal Document)

 

Evolved from our award winning Apollo6 solution, the Apollo4 Power+ is a fully integrated monitor arm capable of handling power, data, and USB in one unit.

Modular in nature and capable of handling 1, 2 or 4 office monitors, the Power+ is the complete solution for enhancing office productivity, ergonomics and activity-based work.

Like all Integ monitor arms, it is 99% recyclable, uses significantly high levels of post-consumer material and is made in Australasia.
